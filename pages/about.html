About

A local-first men's magazine about rooms, tools, uniforms, and rituals.

The Habitant is an independent men's trend magazine built from curated creator sources, local editorial processing, and static publishing.

The magazine studies four areas of modern adult taste: the studio, the sanctuary, the uniform, and intimacy. Each article begins with a source video or creator reference, then becomes a short editorial piece focused on practical taste, restraint, and useful observation.

The site is generated locally and published as static HTML. That keeps the editorial workflow lightweight while preserving a durable archive of sources, products, and essays.
